Anti-Social Integration Chrome Extension

A digital intervention based around the idea that through social integration buttons provided by social networks such as Facebook and Twitter can be used to track your activity across the web.

The Chrome extension makes use of the 'beforeload' listener to check if there are any external resources being loaded from blacklisted sites. The buttons are then replaced with safe buttons.
