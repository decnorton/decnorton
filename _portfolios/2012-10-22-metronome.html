An HTML5 experiment using the new WebAudio API.

Being a drummer, this came out of the necessity to practise whilst away from my kit. I didn't fancy spending what little money I had on a real metronome so I went about looking for free online metronomes. All the ones I found were plastered in ads and rather outdated, so I set about building my own using modern technologies!

You can use the app here: metronome.decnorton.com

View the project on GitHub: github.com/decnorton/metronome
